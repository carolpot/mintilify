---

Signals are real-world changes detected for contacts and companies. They are available via the [Signals API](/signals/overview) and [Webhooks](/webhooks/overview).

## Contact signals

| Signal | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| All signals | `allSignals` | Returns all available signal types for the contact |
| Promotion | `promotion` | The contact changed roles at the same company |
| Company change | `companyChange` | The contact moved to a new employer |

## Company signals

| Signal | Description |
|---|---|
| Funding | A new funding round was announced |
| Headcount growth | The company's employee count grew significantly |
| Technology adoption | The company added a new tool to its tech stack |

## Lookback window

By default, signals are returned for the past **6 months**. Use the `signalsStartDate` parameter (`YYYY-MM-DD`) to extend or restrict the window.
