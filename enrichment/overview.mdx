Enrichment is the process of adding missing or updated data to existing contact or company records. Use the Lusha Enrichment API to fill gaps in your CRM, sharpen outbound targeting, and keep records current as people change roles and companies evolve.

## What you can do with enrichment

- **Complete CRM records** — fill in missing emails, phone numbers, job titles, and company firmographics.
- **Improve outbound accuracy** — reduce bounce rates and wrong-number calls by working with verified, up-to-date contact details.
- **Keep records current** — re-enrich existing records on a schedule to catch role changes, company moves, and updated contact information.

## Real-time vs. bulk enrichment

Lusha offers two modes depending on your workflow:

| Mode | When to use |
|------|-------------|
| **Real-time (GET)** | Enrich a single contact or company on demand — for example, when a new lead enters your CRM. |
| **Bulk (POST)** | Enrich up to 100 records in one request — for example, during a nightly CRM refresh job. |

<Warning>
  **Unified Credits plan required for `revealEmails` and `revealPhones`**

  The `revealEmails` and `revealPhones` parameters are only available on the Unified Credits pricing plan. Using them on any other plan returns a `403 Unauthorized` error.

  When you omit both parameters, the API returns all available emails and phone numbers for the contact by default.
</Warning>
